Welcome to Cat Cocoa!

We provide you with a chance to relax and joy of purr therapy.

We are located at Finn Hill residential neignborhood where you can easily visit on foot if you are already resident here. Our space is fully customised for cats to play, fetch and entertain humans. Our cofee and pastries are locally owned for cafe goers. If you feel like some furry love, or need a break from work-from-home, please do come stop by! The cat launge fee is $7.00 up to an hour, and the capacity is 7. Walk in is welcome with the covid vaccination info below. For ordering drink and food menu only, you do not need to book a visit. When under 7 people in the launge, feel free to come in to the launge. If you want to reserve the spot in advance, please book a visit via the button.

Cafe Hours: Monday - Friday: 9 AM - 6 PM (last visit at 5 PM)
Saturday & Sunday: 10 PM - 4 PM (last visit at 3 PM)

Our Covid-19 Policies

KEEPING OUR CUSTOMERS, KITTIES AND TEAM SAFE ARE OF THE UTMOST IMPORTANCE

We ask the following for those 12 years:

- Vaccination card or photo of vaccination card
- Proof of negative test taken in the last 72 hours

Use hand sanitizer before and after interactin with the kitties. Cover your face during your visit. We ask that all customers wear a mask or facial covering over their mouth and nose when inside our cafe. You may remove your mask temporarily when seated if you are actively eating or drinking.
